---Saving Silverman Review---

Plot

The plot basically consists of this lovestruck dumbass, Silverman, who falls in love with this total she-bitch, Judith (Amanda Peet, who does a brilliant job portraying a bitch). So then Silverman's friends have to save him (hence the title). Anyway, all these hi-jinks eventually involve kidnapping, digging up bodies, blowing up cars, shooting people/animals with tranquilizer darts, cursing, and some other stuff, I forget.

I found the following quote on the internet or somewhere, from a review of Saving Silverman

"Kick boxing deaths and conflicted nuns are not the makings of a good comedy."

Could this guy be more wrong? Of course, the answer is no. Not if a bunch of flying monkeys flew up his butt and started infecting him with some kind of flying-monkey-up-the-butt virus which causes you to be extremely wrong about everything, could this guy be more wrong. In fact, on a scale of one to wrong, one being the wrongest anyone could ever be about anything, and wrong being even wronger, this man has acheived a whopping eleven. Whatever the hell that means.

I can't even remember what my original point was, but the word "Wrong" keeps flashing in my head. Perhaps my brain is critisizing me again.

---Summary---

Guns- 6

I think this movie deserves a reasonable guns score for managing to work in a gun with tranquilizer darts into most of the scenes of this movie.

Explosions- 5

One totally cool explosion where they push a car off a cliff. Plus some others where there isn't really any fire, but by definition, they're explosions.

Breasts- 11

Amanda Peet is almost hot enough to break the 13 that Dude, Where's My Car got, but not quite. Sorry Amanda, but I'll still sleep with you if it'll make you feel better.

Other- 14

Cursing Nuns, Amanda kicking the crap out of every guy in the movie, nachos. I mean, what more do you want? Like a big fat Amanda in a nun costume with nacho hat? Pff, now that's just silly.

Total Score- 9.0

Bottom Line- Funniest movie ever conceived. You will literally go to hell if you don't see this movie. Or if you do. ...One of the two.
